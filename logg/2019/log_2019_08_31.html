A-TWAIN currents¶

Rough processing to get to a time series I can use. Details documented in 1.1.2. in the (current) ATWAIN document.

  • final_clean_and_split_ncfiles.py: Applied thresholds to remove weird edge effects and separate deployments (1) and (2,3).

  • make_depth_average_currents: Computed depth average currents and saved as pickles (depth_avg_currents_2013_2017.p, depth_avg_currents_2013_2017_int.p). The latter interpolated onto 15 min grid. Looks reasonable - use for now.
