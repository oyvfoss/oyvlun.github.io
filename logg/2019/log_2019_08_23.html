ERA Interim data¶

Wrote a download script: atwain/data/other/wind/era_interim/dl_era_winds.py based on the test script. This script should be easy to modify. For now: Downloads 2m temp, 10m uv, and SST for a large square around the ATWAIN area for 2012 through 2017. Downloads to era_interim_for_atwain.nc - was not done when I left.
