Sanity check for value of Q:¶

  • Mean temperature, 0-100 m: 0.64C

  • Mean difference relative to Tmin: 3.10C

\[ \begin{align}\begin{aligned}\begin{split}Q = \int_{-100}^0 c_p\rho (T-T_{min})\ dz\\\end{split}\\Q \sim c_p\ \bar{\rho} \overline{T-T_{min}}\int_{-100}^0\ dz\\Q\sim (4000\ \text{J/kg/C})(1000\ \text{kg/m}^3)(3\ C)(100\ m)\\Q\sim 1.2\times10^{9}\ J m^{-2}\end{aligned}\end{align} \]

Mean value of Q for the whole water column: \(1.27 \times10^{9} J m^{-2}\).

(\(\rightarrow\) Looks good).
