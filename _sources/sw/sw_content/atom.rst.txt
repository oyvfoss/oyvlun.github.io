Atom

*January 2015: Switching to Atom as primary editor, although I am skeptical about the slow startup speed..*

Commands
''''''''

- *Command palette*: **Ctr+Shift+P**
- *Toggle tree side bar*: **Ctr + K**
- *Open/close file*: **Ctr + O/W**
- *Settings pane*:  **Ctr+,**
- *"Fuzzy finder"*: **Ctr + T** 
- *Jump to method*: **Ctr+ R**
