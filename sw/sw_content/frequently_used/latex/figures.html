Figures¶

Some standard templates.

One figure¶

\begin{figure}
\centering
       \includegraphics[scale=0.4]{fig1.png}
       \caption{Text.}
       \label{fig:fig1}
\end{figure}

Two images, two figures¶

\begin{figure}
\centering
\begin{minipage}{0.45\linewidth}
       \includegraphics[scale = 0.6]{fig1}
       \caption{Text}
       \label{fig:1}
\end{minipage}
\quad
\begin{minipage}{0.45\linewidth}
       \includegraphics[scale = 0.55]{fig2}
       \caption{Text}
       \label{fig:2}
\end{minipage}
\end{figure}

Two images, one figure¶

\begin{figure}
     \begin{subfigure}{1\textwidth}
             \centering
             \includegraphics[width=0.8\textwidth]{fig1a.png}
             \subcaption{a)}
     \end{subfigure}\\
     \begin{subfigure}[htb]{1\textwidth}
             \centering
             \includegraphics[width=0.8\textwidth]{fig1b.png}
             \subcaption{b)}
     \end{subfigure}
     \caption{Text}
     \label{fig:1}
\end{figure}
