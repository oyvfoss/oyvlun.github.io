M2: Mean profiles¶

Mean profiles from the M2 mooring (salinity from Concerto sensors, temperature from Concerto and Solo sensors, velocity from 150 kHz ADCP). Gray shading extends \(\pm\) one standard deviation (computed from daily averages) from the mean.¶
