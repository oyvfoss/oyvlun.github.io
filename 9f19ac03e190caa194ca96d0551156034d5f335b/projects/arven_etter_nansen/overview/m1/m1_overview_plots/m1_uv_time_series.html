M1: Velocity and shear time series¶

Velocity from the RDI 150 kHz ADCP on the M1 mooring. Arrows show depth average current vectors (weekly average). Contour plots show velocity component (filtered in timw with a 7-d running mean). Upper panel shows local SIC from UoB AMSR2.¶

Vertical current shear from the RDI 150 kHz ADCP on the M1 mooring, computed from first differencing of the velocity components. Averaged in time with a 3-day running mean. Upper panel shows local SIC from UoB AMSR2.¶
