Surface heat budget¶

Comparing heat flux terms from ERA-5 (interpolated onto the AT200 location) with ocean and sea ice time series.

The following ERA5 variables are used (divided by 3600 hps to obtain the instantaneous fluxes):

  • surface_sensible_heat_flux

  • surface_latent_heat_flux

  • surface_net_thermal_radiation

  • surface_net_solar_radiation

In the figure below, thin lines show dailty means, and thick lines show weekly means.
