ROMS model overview¶

Overview¶

ROMS S800_2014 run.

  • 1300 (\(\xi\)) x 1150 (\(\eta\)) point horizontal grid (even 800 m spacing).

  • 35 vertical layers (should give an average resolution of 5.7 m at 200 m depth).

  • Time coverage is 01.01.2005 to 16.07.2010.

  • Looks like the model includes rather advances sea ice modelling and freshwater fluxes etc. (fresh fjords..).

  • Mixing quantities are exported, as are vorticity terms (I think these are all implicitly computed).

  • Model domain spans a large area around Svalbard, including the continental slope all the way to Frans Jozef Land, much of the Nansen Basin, and the northern Barents Sea:

Example: SST field on a randomly chosen model day.¶

Parameter values

  • Vstretching: 1 (vertical stretching parameter?)

  • Vtransform: 1 (vertical stretching parameter?)

  • theta_s: 8 (vertical stretching parameter?)

  • theta_b: 0.1 (vertical stretching parameter?)

  • dt: 60 (Size of long (baroclinic?) time steps, seconds)

  • dfast: 1 (Size of short (barotropic?) time steps, seconds)

Data¶

Files are located on shared disk /berner (?). Since my VM doesn’t interface very easily with the network, I’ve imported everything to disk (fenrir/data/roms_runs_norseas/S800_2014).

Files are daily averages, each is about 1.5 GB.
