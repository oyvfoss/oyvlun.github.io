Comparing net sea ice drift on different scales¶

Three boundary grids used to estimate net sea ice drift. Code for generating the grids, and the figure: /atwain/code/processing_and_data/sea_ice_drift/create_grid_box.¶

Quick comparison of net ice flux into the three areas using NSIDC (blue) and Ifremer (red) products.¶
